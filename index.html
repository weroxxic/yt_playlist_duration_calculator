How to use:

  1. Paste the URL of a YouTube playlist (e.g., https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAYLIST_ID)
  2. Or just paste the playlist ID (the part after list= in the URL)
  3. Click "Calculate Duration"
  4. Download the complete playlist info as PDF when calculation completes

Note: This tool requires a public playlist or your browser to be logged into YouTube if the playlist is private.
